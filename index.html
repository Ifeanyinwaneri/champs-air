About

The champs' air is a Nigerian airline wholly owned by a private individual that launched commercial services in June 2023. It operates domestic flights from its hub at Portharcourt, with a focus on schedule reliability, on-time performance, and excellent customer service. The airline serves multiple domestic routes and also has a regional destination in Accra, Ghana.

  • Ownership and operation Champs' Air is a limited liability company and a key part of River's State
    economic plan. It operates as an independent enterprise with a world-class airline culture
  • Fleet: The airline operates a fleet that includes Bombardier CRJ900s and Airbus A220-300s
  • Destination: Champs' Air flies to several domestic destinations, including Portharcourt, Abuja, Lagos, Uyo, and Calabar. It also operates a route to Accra, Ghana.

BOOKING

Booking a flight with Champs' Air is a straightforward process, especially when done online.
Step-by-Step Guide to Champs' Air Booking Online:

  1. Visit the Website: Go to flychamps'air.com.
  2. Enter Trip Details: On the homepage, select whether you want a “One Way,” “Round Trip,” or “Multi-City” journey. Enter your departure and arrival airports, travel dates, and the number of passengers.
  3. Search for Flights: Click the “Search Flights” button.
  4. Select Your Flight: The system will display a list of available flights with different timings and fare classes. Choose the flight that best suits your schedule and budget.
  5. Enter Passenger Details: Fill in the required information for all passengers, including full names (as they appear on your ID), contact details, and any loyalty program numbers.
  6. Add Extras: At this stage, you can add optional extras like preferred seating or extra baggage.
  7. Make Payment: Proceed to the secure payment page. Champs' Air accepts various payment methods, including major credit/debit cards (Verve, Visa, MasterCard) and bank transfers.
  8. Confirmation: Once your payment is successful, you will receive a confirmation email containing your e-ticket and booking reference number. Book Champs' Air Flight Now!
